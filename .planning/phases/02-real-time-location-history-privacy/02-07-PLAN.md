## Phase Goal

**As a** family member, **I want to** see my family's live and past location on a shared map and control exactly what I share and with whom, **so that** we can keep each other safe without giving up our privacy.

<objective>
Make the Live Map trustworthy at a glance: family-member pins carry presence (online/offline + last-seen, LOC-02), fade with staleness and show an accuracy-radius circle (LOC-03, D-04), and low-battery alerts surface as a calm amber banner (NOTIF-01). Presence deliberately combines the two signals the backend tracks (connection state + ping recency, D-03) so a pin can read "connected but stale" rather than lying.

Purpose: The UI-SPEC locks exact staleness bands (opacity 1.0/0.7/0.45/0.3 across 4 age bands) and accuracy-circle rendering — this plan implements that contract. The staleness mapping is a pure function (unit-testable without a widget), satisfying the Nyquist sampling for LOC-03.
Output: `staleness.dart` pure mapping, expanded `MemberMapPin` (opacity + accuracy circle), member-detail sheet with presence/last-seen, and the `low_battery_banner`.
</objective>
