## Phase Goal

**As a** family member, **I want to** see my family's live and past location on a shared map and control exactly what I share and with whom, **so that** we can keep each other safe without giving up our privacy.

<objective>
Deliver the Activity tab end-to-end: a member's historical timeline of stays and movements (HIST-01), a route polyline of past travel (HIST-02, D-06), and travel stat tiles — distance, time away, stops (HIST-03). The route is drawn with `google_maps_flutter`'s native `Polyline` (never a custom painter — Don't-Hand-Roll), paired with a scrollable timeline/stats list beneath it (D-06 layout).

Purpose: This is the read-only history slice of the phase; it consumes P04's history/stats endpoints (which are already membership + History-share gated server-side). After this a user can pick a family member and a day and see where they went.
Output: `history_controller`, `history_timeline_screen`, `route_stats_sheet` (map polyline + stats), and the `StatTile`/`TimelineNode` shared widgets, wired into the Activity tab.
</objective>
