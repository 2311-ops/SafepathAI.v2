## Phase Goal

**As a** family member, **I want to** see my family's live and past location on a shared map and control exactly what I share and with whom, **so that** we can keep each other safe without giving up our privacy.

<objective>
Turn the raw ping stream into a readable travel story: a timeline of stops and movements (HIST-01), the ordered points to draw a route polyline (HIST-02, D-06), and summary stats — distance, time away, stop count (HIST-03). Stops are detected by the locked dwell-time mechanism (D-05: 100m / 5 min, via the shared `DwellTimeDefaults` + `GeoMath`), computed at read time over a bounded date range (no ML, no precomputed rollups — the phase's data volume does not justify them).

Purpose: History reads are a second IDOR/privacy surface — they must gate on membership AND the `History` SharingPreference (P03), independent of the LiveLocation toggle. Centralizing stop detection here keeps Phase 4's geofence dwell/hysteresis able to reuse or deliberately diverge from the same constants.
Output: `StopDetection` pure function + `GetLocationHistoryQuery` (timeline + polyline points) + `GetTravelStatsQuery` (distance/time-away/stops) + history GET endpoints on `LocationController`.
</objective>
